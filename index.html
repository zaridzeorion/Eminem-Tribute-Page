How good is eminem?

- Consider the current music industry. Can we even imagine a musician making money by talking about something other than love, relationships or sex?
Take parental neglect and identity issues for example? Well, Eminem became the highest earning musician of 2000’s with such stories.
He didn’t respond to industry needs, instead made his own story wanted.
Obsesssions, crime, parental neglect, family issues, cheating, emotional abuse, bullying, confrontations, gangs. That was his life.
Some made that life an excuse for crime. Eminem made art out of it.

Little summary of his life:

  • 1988-1997: Early career, Infinite and family struggles

  • 1997-1999: Introduction to Slim Shady, The Slim Shady LP and rise to success

  • 2000-2002: The Marshall Mathers LP, lyrical conflicts and The Eminem Show

  • 2003-2007: Encore, more lyrical conflicts and musical hiatus

  • 2008-2009: Comeback, Relapse and Relapse: Refill

  • 2010-2011: Recovery and Bad Meets Evil reunion

  • 2012-2013: The Marshall Mathers LP 2

  • 2014-2016: Shady XV, vinyl box set, and Southpaw

  • 2017-2019: Revival and Kamikaze

  • 2020: Music to Be Murdered By
